Hello!

I'm a third year PhD student at the Laboratoire de Chimie Théorique (LCT) in the group of Julien Toulouse. I have a keen interest in theoretical chemistry, particularly in electronic structure theory. Currently, my focus lies in the development of a relativistic density functional theory (DFT) based on effective quantum electrodynamics. My primary endeavor involves constructing one-dimensional models to gain a comprehensive understanding of how the presence of positrons impacts atomic properties.

Before my PhD I obtained a master degree at the École Normale Supérieure de Lyon (France) about Computational physics and chemistry.

For more personal information you can go on the ``Gallery" section of the website.
