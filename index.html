Hello!

I'm a first year PhD student at the Laboratoire de Chimie Théorique (LCT) in the group of Julien Toulouse. I have a keen interest in theoretical chemistry, especially for electronic structure theory. Currently I am working on the development of a relativistic DFT based on effective quantum electrodynamics. I am mainly developing one dimensional models to have a proper understanding of the effects of the positron in the properties of atoms.

Before my PhD I obtained a master degree at the École Normale Supérieure de Lyon (France) about Computational physics and chemistry.
